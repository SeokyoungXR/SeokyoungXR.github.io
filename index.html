Seok-Young Kim

Hello, I am Seokyoung Kim, a Master's student at the KAIST UVR Lab under the supervision of Prof. Woontack Woo.

My research focuses on understanding a user's physical-world through AR/AI glasses to build intelligent 3D spaces where reality and virtuality are seamlessly connected. In particular, I aim to build MR systems that enable anyone to edit and expand 3D scenes based on their intent, supporting interactive and immersive experiences in everyday life.
